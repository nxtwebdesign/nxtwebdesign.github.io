Frequently Asked Questions


Web Design FAQ


  1. What will my web address be for my website, can I choose it?

    Yes you can choose your own web address as long as its available. Commonly, its “yourbusinessname.com.au” however we can work with you in finding the best web address for your business name.


  2. Is there a limit to the number of pages I can have on my website?

    There is no limit to how many pages you can have on your website. But for every additional page you require on Standard or Plus plan, a $50 fee will apply.


  3. Can I choose the colours for my website?

    Yes you can choose the colour theme you want for your website, or we can give you our advice on what colours would suit best.


  4. When will my website be finished?

    Your website will be completed within 7 working days.


  5. Who owns my website and domain name?

    You do. The website and your domain name are your property and can be taken with you if you ever choose to move to a different host.


  6. Can I upload my own photos on my website?

    Yes we can upload your own photos to your website.
    Simply just email us the photos as an attachment and we can add them.


  7. How do I add or change things on my website?

    Simply send us an email with what you want changed or request a call back and we can discuss this over the phone.
    If you selected the Standard plan, then you are entitled to 1 hour free support which includes website updates.
    If you selected the Plus plan, then you are entitled to 2 hours of support which includes website updates.
    Any additional time required after your allocated amount for updates will incur a $60 per hour fee.


  8. How do I pay for my website?

    You can pay directly through our website using your Credit Card or PayPal account.
    Setup costs are paid upfront with the support cost being paid monthly, which we will send you an invoice.


  9. Do you offer email addresses?

    Yes, we can organise an email address for you upon request. Each plan comes with 1 free email address that we setup for you. If you wish to have more than 1 email address, we can set you up with additional email addresses for $10 each.


  10. I currently own my domain name. Can I use it with my service?

    Yes, you can. Call or email us and we'll take care of it for you.


  11. My business does not have a logo. Can you create one for me?

    Yes we can. For the one-time charge of $250 we will build you a professional logo tailor-made for your specific requests.


  12. Will my website appear on search engines such as Google?

    Yes, check out our SEO page for more information.


  13. Where will my website be hosted?

    We host all our websites through VentraIP Australia, with an Uptime Guarantee SLA of 99.5%. We trust in using VentraIP Australian servers to host all our websites.


  14. Am I under any type of contract, what if I want to leave?

    As mentioned above, you own your website and domain name so you can move your website elsewhere (or close it down if you wish) whenever you desire. You are not under any form of contract.

    We will send you a monthly invoice. Pay the invoice if you wish to keep receiving hosting, support and domain renewal or simply let us know if you do not wish to continue with our services.


SEO FAQ



  1. What does SEO stand for?

    Search Engine Optimisation.


  2. Why do I require SEO?

    SEO is important because it allows your site to be seen across all search engines.


  3. When will I see my website on page 1 in Google?

    It will take some time to achieve page 1 rankings for certain pages on your website, however with ongoing work involving link building, on-page optimisation and local directory listings this can be achieved.


  4. Can I have more than 10 keywords offered on the Plus plan?

    Yes you can. For every additional keyword you require a fee of $25 will apply.


  5. How can I track my website's progress?

    We will send you a comprehensive report on your websites analytics each month.


  6. Can I cancel this plan anytime?

    Yes, just simply send us an email requesting a cancellation.
